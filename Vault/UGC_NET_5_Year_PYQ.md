## 🟢 Year 2024-2025 (Latest Patterns & Expected)
*The recent trend focuses heavily on Assertion-Reasoning, Chronological Ordering, and deeply analytical questions on Indian Foreign Policy and Public Administration.*

**Q1. Assertion (A):** The 'Act East' Policy of India is a strategic shift from the earlier 'Look East' Policy.
**Reason (R):** 'Act East' emphasizes not only economic integration but also security cooperation with ASEAN and Indo-Pacific countries.

- [ ] (B) Both A and R are true, but R is NOT the correct explanation of A.
- [ ] (C) A is true, but R is false.
- [ ] (D) A is false, but R is true.
**Answer:** (A)

- [ ] (B) 4, 2, 3, 1
- [ ] (C) 2, 4, 1, 3
- [ ] (D) 4, 2, 1, 3
